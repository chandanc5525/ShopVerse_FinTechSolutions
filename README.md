# **ShopVerse – E-commerce Revenue & Customer Intelligence Dashboard**

---

## Business Background

ShopVerse is a fast-growing Indian e-commerce platform offering products across categories such as Electronics, Fashion, and Home & Living.

Over the past 18 months, ShopVerse has experienced:

---

```
1. High website traffic growth (40% YoY)
2. Rapid customer acquisition through digital marketing
3. Expansion into Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities

However, despite this growth, the leadership team has identified key challenges:

1. Revenue growth is inconsistent
2. Customer retention is declining
3. Cart abandonment rates are increasing
4. Marketing spend is rising without proportional ROI
```
---

## Problem Statement

Q. How can ShopVerse improve revenue consistency, increase customer retention, and optimize marketing spend using data-driven insights?”

---

## Role as Data Analyst

```
Your responsibility is to build a Power BI dashboard that enables leadership to:

1. Monitor business performance
2. Identify customer behavior patterns
3. Take actionable decisions
```
