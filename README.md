## Dataset Overview

This dataset represents a large-scale simulation of financial transactions and business operations within the Indian FinTech ecosystem.

```
Total Records: 598,789
Total Columns: 55+
Time Period: 2020 – 2025
Region: India

Note: The dataset captures multi-dimensional data including transactions, customers, lending, fraud, funding, engagement, and operational performance.

Time Intelligence

transaction_id: Unique transaction identifier
date: Transaction date
year / quarter / month / week / day: Time breakdown
day_of_week: Name of the day
is_weekend: Weekend indicator
financial_year: Financial year format

Company Intelligence

company_name: FinTech company name
company_type: Business type (NBFC, Lending, etc.)
hq_city / country: Location details
company_founded_year: Year established
total_employees: Workforce size
market_share_percent: Market share
active_users: Total active users

Transaction Details

transaction_amount: Transaction value
transaction_type: Payment mode
transaction_status: Success/Failure/Pending
payment_channel: App/Web/POS
device_type: Mobile/Desktop
merchant_category: Business category
transaction_currency: Currency (INR)
cross_border_flag: International transaction indicator

Financial Metrics

revenue / profit: Earnings
processing_fee / commission_fee: Charges applied
gst_amount: Tax component
net_revenue: Final revenue
cost_of_acquisition: Customer acquisition cost

Customer Intelligence

customer_id: Unique customer ID
customer_age / age_group / gender: Demographics
customer_segment: Retail/SME/Corporate
customer_region / city: Location
customer_tenure_months: Relationship duration
avg_monthly_transactions: Usage frequency
customer_lifetime_value: Total customer value

Credit & Lending

interest_rate: Loan interest rate

default_flag: Loan default indicator
credit_score: Creditworthiness

Risk & Fraud Analytics

fraud_flag: Fraud indicator
fraud_type: Type of fraud
risk_score: Risk rating
fraud_detection_time_sec: Detection time
chargeback_flag: Chargeback indicator
dispute_status: Dispute resolution status

Funding & Strategy

funding_round: Investment stage

valuation: Company valuation
growth_strategy: Strategic approach
expansion_region: Target region

Product & Engagement

app_sessions: Number of sessions

bounce_rate: Drop-off rate
feature_used: Product feature
churn_flag: Customer churn indicator
retention_rate: Customer retention

Operational Metrics

app_latency_ms: System latency
api_response_time_ms: API performance
success_rate / failure_rate: Transaction performance
downtime_minutes: System downtime
server_region: Server location

## **Sprint Plan – Data Analytics Execution Roadmap**

---
```
**Assigned By:** Analytics Manager
**Team:** Data Analytics Team
**Project:** FinTech Business Intelligence – ShopVerse FinTech Solutions Pvt Ltd

---

## Sprint 1: Data Understanding & KPI Definition

Objective:
Gain a comprehensive understanding of the dataset and establish key business metrics aligned with organizational goals.

Tasks Assigned:

* Perform detailed exploration of dataset structure, relationships, and data types
* Identify and define critical KPIs, including:

  * Revenue Growth
  * Transaction Success Rate
  * Fraud Rate
  * Customer Churn Rate
* Formulate key business questions to guide analysis and dashboard development

---

## Sprint 2: Data Cleaning & Transformation

Objective:
Prepare a clean, structured, and analysis-ready dataset for downstream reporting and visualization.

Tasks Assigned:

* Identify and handle missing or inconsistent data
* Standardize and convert data types where required
* Create derived/calculated fields such as:

  * Profit Margin
  * Customer Segmentation
* Develop a structured data model suitable for Power BI or SQL-based querying

---

## Sprint 3: Dashboard Development

Objective:
Design and develop interactive dashboards to provide actionable business insights.

Tasks Assigned:

* Build the following dashboards:

  * Executive Overview Dashboard (KPIs & trends)
  * Customer Analytics Dashboard (segmentation & behavior)
  * Fraud & Risk Dashboard (fraud patterns & risk indicators)
  * Transaction Performance Dashboard (success/failure analysis)
* Ensure dashboards are interactive, user-friendly, and business-focused

---

## Sprint 4: Root Cause Analysis (RCA)

Objective:
Identify and analyze underlying factors impacting business performance.

Tasks Assigned:

* Conduct RCA on the following areas:

  * Declining transaction success rate
  * Increase in fraudulent activities
  * Rising customer churn
  * Revenue or profitability fluctuations
* Use data-driven techniques to validate findings

---

## Sprint 5: Business Insights & Strategy

Objective:
Translate analytical findings into actionable business recommendations.

Tasks Assigned:

* Identify key trends, patterns, and anomalies in the data
* Provide recommendations to:

  * Improve transaction success rates
  * Reduce fraud risk
  * Increase customer retention
  * Optimize operational efficiency
* Highlight potential growth opportunities across regions and segments

---

## Sprint 6: Documentation (BRD & FED)

Objective:
Develop professional documentation for business and technical stakeholders.

Tasks Assigned:

### BRD (Business Requirement Document)

* Define business objectives and scope
* Document KPIs and success criteria
* Identify stakeholders and use cases

### FED (Front-End Design Document)

* Design dashboard layout and structure
* Define filters, visuals, and interactions
* Ensure alignment with user experience best practices
